Why This Site Exists

Many readers stop reading for months because they do not know what to read next. There are too many choices, and popular recommendations do not always match a person’s mood. Keep Reading is meant to make that decision easier by guiding readers toward books that feel emotionally right, engaging, and genuinely worth their time.

Reader Personas

Emotional Reader

Loves strong character development, emotional storylines, and books that stay with them after the last page.

Casual Reader

Wants a book that is easy to start, enjoyable from the beginning, and hard to put down.

Tired College Student

Needs something quick to get into but still meaningful, comforting, and worth the time.
