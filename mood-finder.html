Find a book based on your mood

Sometimes the hardest part of reading is not reading itself, but choosing what to read next. This page helps readers choose books based on the mood they are in so they can return to reading without feeling overwhelmed.

Choose Your Reading Mood

Comforting

Best for readers who want something soft, warm, familiar, and emotionally safe. These are the books that feel gentle and reassuring.

Emotional

Best for readers who want moving stories, layered characters, and books that leave a strong emotional impact.

Fast-Paced

Best for readers who want something exciting, gripping, and easy to keep reading once they begin.

How This Page Helps

Instead of only looking at what is popular, this page encourages readers to think about what kind of emotional experience they want from a book. That makes choosing a new book feel more personal and much less stressful.
