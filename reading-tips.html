Simple ways to get out of a reading slump

A reading rut does not always mean you do not like reading anymore. Sometimes it only means you need a different kind of book, a different pace, or less pressure.

Tips That Help

  • Choose based on your mood instead of only what is popular.
  • Start with a shorter book so reading feels less overwhelming.
  • Reread a favorite if you want something familiar and comforting.
  • Read one chapter at a time instead of aiming for long sessions.
  • Pick a book with strong emotion or quick pacing to rebuild momentum.
